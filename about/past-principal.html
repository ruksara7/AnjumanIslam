Distinguished Past Principals

Prof. Abdul Rehman

First Principal (1985-1995)
Led the inaugural batch of 120 students. Established academic systems and introduced Arts & Commerce curricula. Built strong faculty foundation.

Key Achievements:

  • First graduating batch (1988)
  • Faculty recruitment (35 teachers)
  • Library foundation (5000 books)
  • MGKVP permanent affiliation

Dr. Fatima Begum

Principal (1995-2003)
First female principal. Science faculty pioneer. Student strength doubled to 350+. Emphasized co-curricular activities and women's education.

Key Achievements:

  • B.Sc programs launched (1996)
  • First science laboratories
  • Women's cell established
  • Annual sports meet started

Prof. Khalid Ahmed

Principal (2003-2012)
Postgraduate expansion leader. Infrastructure development phase. Academic excellence reached 90% pass percentage consistently.

Key Achievements:

  • MA/M.Sc programs (2005)
  • New academic block
  • Computer laboratory
  • NAAC preparation started

Dr. Naseem Raza

Principal (2012-2022)
NAAC accreditation champion. Digital transformation leader. Placement cell strengthened with 85% placement record achieved.

Key Achievements:

  • NAAC B++ accreditation (2020)
  • Smart classrooms implemented
  • 95% placement assistance
  • IQAC fully functional
