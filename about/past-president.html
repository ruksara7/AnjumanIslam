Our Esteemed Past Presidents

Dr. Janab Ahmed Khan

Founder President (1985-1995)
Visionary founder who established Anjuman Islam College with 120 students. Secured permanent affiliation with MGKVP and laid foundation for academic excellence.

Key Contributions:

  • College establishment & initial infrastructure
  • First batch of 120 UG students
  • Land acquisition & building construction
  • Arts & Commerce faculty introduction

Prof. Mohammed Iqbal

President (1995-2005)
Expanded academic programs to include Science faculty. Student strength grew to 500+. Established library and laboratories.

Key Contributions:

  • B.Sc programs launched
  • Science laboratories established
  • Library with 15,000+ books
  • First placement cell

Dr. Syed Hassan

President (2005-2015)
Postgraduate programs introduced. Infrastructure development phase. NAAC accreditation preparation initiated.

Key Contributions:

  • MA/M.Sc programs started
  • New academic block constructed
  • Computer lab & auditorium
  • IQAC formation

Janab Rahim Khan

President (2015-2023)
Led NAAC accreditation process. Digital transformation. Placement record improved to 85%.

Key Contributions:

  • NAAC B++ accreditation
  • Digital learning platforms
  • WiFi campus & smart classrooms
  • 95% placement assistance
